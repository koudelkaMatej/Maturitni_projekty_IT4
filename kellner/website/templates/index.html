WELCOME TO UBERMOSH

Cut bullets. Dodge death. Survive the Rebirth.

About The Game

Ubermosh Rebirth is a fast-paced top-down bullet-hell arcade game inspired by the cult classic Ubermosh by Walter Machado.

Wield a blade that can cut through bullets and guns that shred enemies. You have 60 seconds to extract. Will you survive the onslaught, or become just another corpse in the arena?

Field Guide

Gamemodes

  • Normal - Balanced. 4 HP. Sword & Random Guns.
  • Reflect - HARD MODE. 6 HP. No Sword Kills. You must REFLECT bullets to kill.
  • Gunner - EASY MODE. 2 HP. Start with a powerful gun.

Enemies

  • Fast Shooter - Fires high-velocity rounds.
  • Dual Shooter - Double trouble.
  • Shotgun - Spread fire. Keep distance.
  • Sword - Rusher. No gun, just speed.
